The Vision

The 412 Initiative equips people to provide vital social, economic, and physical aid to underdeveloped global communities. We also trains and educates those people for this ministry with the ultimate goal of providing Hope to a hurting world.

Our Mission!

The Mission

At The 412 Initiative, we are passionate about our mission to provide sustained Hope. We facilitate positive social, economic, and physical advancement in underprivileged global communities. With our help, foreign communities will be better equipped to make a positive impact and provide Hope for generations to come!
